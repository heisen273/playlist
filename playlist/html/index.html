About the App

Playlist Generator Bot is a Telegram bot that creates personalized playlists based on your Spotify and YouTube listening history. It intelligently curates playlists to match your musical taste, ensuring every playlist is a new adventure in music.

Our bot simplifies playlist creation, transforming your music experience with just a few clicks. It's perfect for discovering new music or enjoying your favorite tunes in a fresh playlist.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Playlist Generator Bot accesses your Spotify and YouTube history solely for playlist creation. We respect your privacy and ensure that your data is neither stored nor shared beyond this purpose.

We only access your Spotify and YouTube listening history to create playlists for you. We do not store, share, or use your data for any other purposes. Your listening history remains confidential and is only used for enhancing your playlist experience.
